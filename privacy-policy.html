1. Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information of both our agents and their clients. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information through the International Wealth Builders platform (“Service”). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Agent Information

When you register or use the Service, we may collect:

  • Name, email, phone number, and contact details
  • Agency/organization affiliation
  • Login credentials and account activity logs
  • Training progress, performance data, and communications

2.2 Client Information (entered by agents)

  • Name, phone number, email, and address
  • Policy numbers and related notes
  • Appointment schedules and communication records

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device and browser type
  • IP address and location
  • Usage statistics and interaction logs

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the Service
  • Maintain secure access controls
  • Deliver training, organizational tools, and communication features
  • Support compliance with insurance and financial industry regulations
  • Improve functionality and troubleshoot issues
  • Communicate with users regarding updates, support, or compliance notices

4. Data Ownership & Responsibilities

  • Client Data: Agents retain ownership of their client data. We act as a data processor and only store/process client information as part of providing the Service.
  • Agent/Agency Data: Training materials, performance data, and organizational structures are Company property.

Agents are responsible for ensuring their handling of client data complies with applicable state and federal laws, including privacy and insurance regulations.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell client or agent data. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  1. With service providers who support our platform (e.g., hosting, security, cloud storage), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  2. As required by law, regulation, subpoena, or government request.
  3. To protect our legal rights, property, or the safety of users.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect data, including encryption and access controls. However, no system is 100% secure. Users are responsible for maintaining secure passwords and device access.

7. Data Retention

  • Agent accounts and data are retained while the account is active.
  • Upon termination, data may be retained for legal, regulatory, or backup purposes, after which it will be securely deleted.

8. User Rights (State-Specific)

Depending on your state of residence, you may have certain rights, including:

  • Access: Request a copy of personal data collected.
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of personal data, subject to legal/regulatory retention requirements.
  • Opt-Out: Request that data not be shared with third parties for marketing (note: we do not sell data).

Requests can be submitted via contact@iamawealthbuilder.org. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling requests.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. International Users

This Service is operated in the United States. If you access from outside the U.S., you consent to the transfer and storage of your information in the U.S.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting a notice within the Service or by email. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
